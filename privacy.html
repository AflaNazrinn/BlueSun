Privacy Policy

At Bluesun International LLC, we are committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all visitors, customers, suppliers, and business partners. We understand the importance of personal information and take appropriate steps to ensure that your data is collected, stored, and used responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage the information you provide when interacting with our website, products, and services.

Information Collection

When you visit our website or contact us, we may collect information such as your name, company name, phone number, email address, location, and any details submitted through inquiry forms or quotation requests. We may also collect technical information such as browser type, device details, IP address, and website usage data to help improve performance and user experience.

Purpose of Data Use

The information collected is used to respond to your inquiries, process requests, provide quotations, improve our services, and maintain effective communication with you. It also helps us understand customer needs, enhance website functionality, and deliver better solutions tailored to your business requirements.

Data Security

Bluesun International LLC maintains strict security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. We continuously review our systems and procedures to ensure information remains secure and handled in a professional manner.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared with trusted service providers, logistics partners, or legal authorities when necessary for business operations, service delivery, or compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to improve browsing experience and monitor website traffic. Cookies help us understand user preferences and optimize website performance. Users may choose to disable cookies through their browser settings if preferred.

External Links

Please note that our website may contain links to third-party websites for additional information or services. Bluesun International LLC is not responsible for the privacy practices, security, or content of external websites.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your personal information, ask for corrections, or request deletion where applicable. We will make reasonable efforts to respond to such requests in accordance with legal and operational requirements.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices, legal obligations, or website improvements. Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the way your information is handled, please contact Bluesun International LLC through our official communication channels.
